7. PERMISSION VISIBILITY TESTING
================================

Add a permissions preview for each role.

For every role show:

* Pages they can access
* Data they can view
* Actions they can perform
* Actions they cannot perform

Example:
Employee:

* Can view assigned tasks only
* Can update assigned campaign status
* Cannot view profit margin
* Cannot approve campaigns
* Cannot manage users

Campaign Leader:

* Can view assigned campaigns
* Can assign tasks to team members
* Can review influencer lists
* Cannot view full company finance

Department Manager:

* Can view department campaigns
* Can approve campaign workflow
* Can view team performance
* Cannot manage full system settings unless allowed

Company Owner/Admin:

* Can view and control everything
